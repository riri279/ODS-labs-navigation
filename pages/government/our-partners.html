Our Partners

Making government work better for people in the digital age.

Code for Canada

Code for Canada is a national nonprofit that connects government innovators with the tech and design community. Our programs enable governments to deliver better digital public services and empower communities to solve civic challenges using technology and design.

Why Code for Canada?

Residents and businesses in Canada expect to interact with government as easily and seamlessly as they do with a myriad of other 21st century digital services. Code for Canada helps governments meet those expectations by adopting new tools, new methods and new forms of collaboration.

By bringing government and civic tech together, we can facilitate knowledge sharing, break down siloes and work together to build the digital services residents expect — and deserve.

Our Programs

Code for Canada offers three, mutually-reinforcing programs designed to deliver better digital services for residents, build digital capacity in government and grow the country’s civic tech ecosystem.

The Communitech Hub

Communitech helps tech companies start, grow and succeed. That’s our mission, our mantra, our reason for being. Everything we do ties back to collaboration and helping—values that run deep in our organization.

It’s the place we call home and a clubhouse for the Waterloo Region tech community. The Communitech Hub is 80,000+ square feet dedicated to world-leading collaboration and innovation. We bring together key players—from startups and global brands, to government agencies, academic institutions, tech incubators and accelerators—to create an environment like no other.

Focused on fuelling job creation and company growth, the Hub keeps buzzing well beyond typical business hours. It plays host to events, special guests and other shenanigans, serves as collaborative office space for a mix of tech and non-tech companies, and as a drop-in space for guests.

Learn more about The Communitech Hub

Open Government

Open Government is a global movement that is transforming how people and governments engage with one another.

We’re embracing this new way of working so we can:

  • improve programs and services
  • transform the way we work
  • better connect with the people we serve

We’re dedicated to opening up government through policy changes, education and community outreach.

We want to make it easier for people to participate in, and provide input on, government decisions by bringing our work into the open.
