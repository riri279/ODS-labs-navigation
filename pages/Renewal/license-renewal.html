By clicking ''I agree'', you confirm that you have read and understood the information below:

I confirm that I live in Ontario:

  • Ontario is, and will continue to be, my primary home. I will be in Ontario for at least 153 days (approximately five months) in any twelve-month period to keep my OHIP Coverage
  • ServiceOntario may contact me to confirm that Ontario is my primary home and if contacted, I will respond within 30 days to maintain health coverage

I understand that my information will be checked:

  • I am completing this renewal application for myself, using the information on my own cards
  • It is against the law to knowingly provide incorrect information for anything related to this application
  • If there is any change in personal information, I will inform the Ministry of Health and Long-Term Care and the Ministry of Transportation, through ServiceOntario
  • My health card and driver’s licence information will be collected by ServiceOntario for the Ministry of Transportation and the Ministry of Health and Long-term Care. This collection is allowed under the authority of section 205 of the Highway Traffic Act and in accordance with the Personal Health Information Protection Act, 2004. Learn more about the collection of personal information.

ServiceOntario respects your right to privacy and values your trust. Read more about ServiceOntario’s commitment to privacy and security.
